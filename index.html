Classic Blueberry Pie

"Best blueberry pie ever! I made the filling with fresh, frozen wild Maine blueberries, thickened with corn starch, reduced sugar to 1/3 cup and added lemon zest instead of juice. Mix frozen blueberries with sugar, cornstarch and cinnamon and let sit for 15 minutes. When berries begin to absorb dry ingredients, add lemon zest, assemble the pie and bake. Brush egg white over the top crust and sprinkle sugar before baking. My whole family loved it. Delish with vanilla ice cream."

BY DEXTERROSE
